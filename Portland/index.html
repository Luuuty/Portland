Core Capabilities

Built for heavy‑duty breakdowns.

Mobile Diagnostics

Computer and manual diagnostics for diesel engines, aftertreatment, ABS, and multiplex systems. Forced regens on‑site.

Air & Brake Systems

Lines, dryers, chambers, valves, slack adjusters, air leaks tracked and sealed. Road‑ready brake checks.

Steel and aluminum welding, brackets, steps, exhaust, catwalks, and light fabrication performed roadside or yardside.
